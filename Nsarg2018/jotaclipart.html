Northampton Scout Amateur Radio Group

Jamboree on the Air Clip-Art

The first Jamboree on the Air was held in 1958 following the success of the amateur radio station at the World Scout Jamboree at Sutton Coldfield in 1957. The Northampton Scout Amateur Radio Group has taken part in Jamboree on the Air since 1981. Each year a JOTA logo is produced and these may be downloaded from the JOTA Clip-Art section.
