Digital Citizen Stage 2
Badge Requirements

Complete all of the items below to achieve Stage 2, showing that you have thought about the potential risks and how to stay safe for each activity
  1. Plan and then create a piece of digital media. It could be artwork or a photograph that you alter using creative tools, music, animation, CAD (Computer Aided Design) or a 3D sculpture.
  2. Send or reply to someone with a short email and include an attachment.
  3. Download photos or other media from a camera or smart card.
  4. Open and save a document then share it using a cloud service (like Google Docs or Microsoft Sharepoint) or a USB stick
  5. Create a piece of digital media. It could be artwork, a photograph, music or animation
  6. Use the internet for research:
    • Decide on an area of interest.
    • Use two different search engines to find three websites you think are reliable.
    • Collect information by printing the pages or saving as PDF documents.
    • Select relevant information by copying text or an image and pasting into a document.
    • Dislpay your findings as a poster or a short report.
