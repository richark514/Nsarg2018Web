Digital Citizen Stage 4
Badge Requirements

Complete all of the items below to achieve Stage 4, showing that you have thought about the potential risks and how to stay safe for each activity
  1. Create a portfolio of digital media. It might include artwork or a photograph that you alter using creative tools, music, animation, CAD (Computer Aided Design) or 3D sculpture.
  2. Create a film, video, stop-motion animation or podcast and share it using a suitable media sharing tool.
  3. Create a social network profile for your section, a band, local interest group or something similar.
  4. Alternatively you could make a small website that can host content, like the film you made in step 2, photos, poetry or information about your local area.
  5. Use the internet for research:
  6. Choose a local, national, community or Scouting issue, or something from the news or current affairs.
  7. Collect information from different sources, such as spreadsheets, databases, online news services and ‘open access’ data sources.
  8. Put your information together in a structured way, for example grouping similar information. Make sure you know where each piece of information comes from.
  9. Select the information you think is most appropriate and reliable.
  10. Create a multi-page website with your information and make it public. Present your information in a variety of ways – you could use infographics, images or graphs.
  11. Share your website with a wide audience
  12. Explain your sources of information and why you picked out what you did.
  13. Get some feedback on what you have done and make changes to improve your website based on that feedback.
